Description

The DEFY EXTREME JUNGLE is the epitome of classic watchmaking. This model features a white dial with Roman numerals, complemented by a fluted bezel in White. The Jubilee bracelet, created in 1945, represents both robustness and comfort.

The DEFY EXTREME JUNGLE is a new-generation movement entirely developed and manufactured by Zenith. This self-winding mechanical movement is at the forefront of watchmaking technology.
